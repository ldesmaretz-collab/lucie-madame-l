Case study

Objective

Boost Polaroid Eyewear's awareness and business by activating families on the coast during summer.

Context

Polaroid used children as prescribers to create visibility across beach clubs and optician points of sale.

Lucie's role

  • Built the full summer activation strategy: field, opticians and digital.
  • Coordinated 90 beach clubs and 66 coastal cities.
  • Produced visual assets for clubs and stores.
  • Rolled out the partnership with 66 opticians and a geo-targeted sponsored campaign.
