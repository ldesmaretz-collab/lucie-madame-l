Decathlon x Club Mickey - Summer Football

National activation, family, sport, coastline

Year2022

Case study

Objective

Position football as the key summer sport, engage families on holiday and introduce children aged 3 to 12 to the practice.

Context

Decathlon wanted a fun, large-scale and easy-to-roll-out experience across the coastline during the summer season.

Lucie's role

  • Designed and produced the national Decathlon x Club Mickey activation.
  • Turned Club Mickey locations into Decathlon mini stadiums.
  • Rolled out matches, challenges and field animations.
  • Created the Decathlon Football passport, branded content and digital amplification.
