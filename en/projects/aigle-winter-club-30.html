Case study

Objective

Celebrate 30 years of Aigle ready-to-wear and anchor the brand in a desirable lifestyle and festival-inspired universe.

Context

For the Saint-Germain-des-Prés boutique opening and the Fall-Winter 2019 collection, Aigle wanted an immersive and emotional experience.

Lucie's role

  • Created the Winter Club 30 concept: turning the flagship into a festive immersive house.
  • Coordinated the PR and influencer experience: VIP evening, content and press coverage.
  • Set up a phygital contest with a street marketing operation in three cities.
  • Monitored the digital media plan and PR coverage.
