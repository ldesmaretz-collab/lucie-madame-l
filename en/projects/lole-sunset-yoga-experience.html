Case study

Objective

Create an exclusive wellness experience on the Majestic Cannes pontoon to strengthen Lolë's visibility on the French Riviera.

Context

A lifestyle activation designed as a signature moment: sunset yoga, gifting, guest welcome, atmosphere and social media content.

Lucie's role

  • Designed and produced the full event.
  • Managed art direction, scenography, ticketing and registrations.
  • Coordinated logistics and dedicated communications.
  • Built the experience journey: class, gifting, sunset aperitif, photo and video capture.

Key figures

60 participants on the Majestic pontoon - sold out

60 mats and 60 tote bags distributed

1-hour sunset yoga class with certified instructor

Premium visibility in an iconic Cannes location
