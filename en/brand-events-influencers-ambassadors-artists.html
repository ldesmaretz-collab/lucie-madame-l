Method

Relevant casting before visible casting.

Studio Madame-L defines the objective, builds selection criteria, reviews profile fit, manages conversations and secures operational elements: deliverables, timing, fees, rights, content approvals and on-site presence.

This approach draws on experiences delivered for Balmain Paris x Chivas, Aigle, Adidas x Foot Locker, Polaroid, Lolë and Decathlon x Club Mickey.

Questions

Event, influencer, ambassador or artist: how should the brief be framed?

01

What brand events can Studio Madame-L produce?

Launches, press and influencer experiences, pop-up stores, brand evenings, field activations, partner events and premium formats, from concept through coordination.

02

How does influencer or ambassador casting work?

Casting begins with objectives, audience and brand values, then reviews editorial fit, audience relevance, credibility, potential conflicts and collaboration terms.

03

Can Studio Madame-L connect artists with brands?

Studio Madame-L can research profiles, introduce artists or their representatives and coordinate negotiation, rights and project integration within the agreed scope.

04

Can one assignment combine an event, artists and influencers?

Yes. One programme can combine event concept, casting, ambassadors, artists, content, invitations and on-site coordination to create one coherent experience.
